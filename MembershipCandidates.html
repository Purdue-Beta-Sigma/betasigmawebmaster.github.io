Our Membership Candidates

Meet some of our future sisters!

Ava Wildoner

Ava Wildoner is a Freshman majoring in Interior Design! She is from West Lafayette, Indiana and Purdue has always been a big part of her life. She plays the Alto Sax in Campus band, and Boiler Box Band. She is also actively involved with our VMT's, such as Student Life, OPS, and Outreach! Outside of band she loves hanging out with friends, journaling, discovering new music, and going on adventures!

Haleigh Hoffman

Haleigh is a freshmen from Bloomington, Illinois majoring in psychological sciences and history. They play flute and piccolo in Box Band, GABS, and Campus Band. They are a proud member of the Velvet Bowties family. In their free time, Haleigh enjoys reading, crocheting, playing card games, and spending time with their friends.
