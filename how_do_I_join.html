Become a sister!

Event Dates

Actual rush events will not begin until the Spring semester. Until that time the sisters of Tau Beta Sigma will be hosting events to interact with possible prospectives. Here is a list of upcoming events. If you think there is an event that is not on here feel free to check our calendar

Sister Stipulations

To be a sister you must be enrolled in any band or orchestra class at Purdue University, and you must have a GPA above a 2.5 to join and must maintain a 2.0 to stay active.

Why Rush?

If you enjoy fun, music, friends, and more music then Tau Beta Sigma is for you! We work closely with all the directors in the Purdue Bands and Orchestras department to help better the program overall. We only do projects that directly benefit the department, which makes the work that much more meaningful because we see the end goal. The best part about Tau Beta Sigma is the bond of sisterhood. The Beta Sigma chapter will have many lifelong friends from across the country for everyone. Friends, fun, and music is what makes Tau Beta Sigma what we are, and if that sounds fun to you then please come to one of the events, we would love to meet you!

Questions?

If you have any questions about the Beta Sigma chapter of Tau Beta Sigma or what we do then please don't hesitate to contact us in any of the ways listed on the contact us page, or you can email our Vice President of Membership, Isaak Gutierrez, at gutie206@purdue.edu.
