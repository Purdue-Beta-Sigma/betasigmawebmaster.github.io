How do I join?

Become a sister!

Join our email list!

If you are at all interested in join Tau Beta Sigma then please fill out the following google form. It asks for your name, which is always good to know; your Purdue email, which will allow us to send you updates on new events and rush events in the spring; and how you heard about Tau Beta Sigma, which will help us determine the best methods for reaching prospectives. This form is not a binding contract that you must rush Tau Beta Sigma, this form is just to make things easier on both sides.

Sister Stipulations

To be a sister you must be enrolled in any band or orchestra class at Purdue University, and you must have a GPA above a 2.5 to join and must maintain a 2.0 to stay active.

Why Rush?

If you enjoy fun, music, friends, and more music then Tau Beta Sigma is for you! We work closly with all the directors in the Purdue Bands and Orchestras department to help better the program overall. We only do projects that directly benefit the department, which makes the work that much more meaningful because we see the end goal. The best part about Tau Beta Sigma is the bond of sisterhood. The Beta Sigma chapter will have many lifelong friends from acorss the country for everyone. Friends, fun, and music is what makes Tau Beta Sigma what we are, and if that sounds fun to you then please come to one of the events, we would love to meet you!

Questions?

If you have any questions about the Beta Sigma chapter of Tau Beta Sigma or what we do then please don't hesitate to contact us in any of the ways listed on the contact us page or you can email our Vice President of Membership, Mary Rose Florian, at mfloria@purdue.edu.
